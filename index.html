Article on: "Financial literacy"

"02.03.2023"

Financial literacy is an important skill that everyone should possess. It is the ability to understand and effectively manage personal finances, including budgeting, saving, investing, and debt management. Unfortunately, many people lack the knowledge and skills necessary to make informed financial decisions, leading to financial insecurity and hardship. In this article, we will explore the importance of financial literacy and provide tips on how to improve it.

Book Summary : “The Joy of Compounding”,

"02.03.2023"

The book is written by Gautam Baid, a value investor and lifelong learner. He shares his insights and lessons from over 200 influential figures in history, such as Warren Buffett, Charlie Munger, Benjamin Franklin, and Albert Einstein. He shows how to apply their wisdom and strategies to the areas of business, investing, and decision making. The book is a celebration of the value investing discipline and a guide to achieving an in-depth understanding of the world around us, including ourselves. The book aims to inspire readers to pursue lifelong learning and compounding, both financially and intellectually.
